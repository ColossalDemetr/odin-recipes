French fries

Home

Description

French fries are thin pieces of potato cooked until golden and crispy. They are a popular side dish and are often served with ketchup or sauce.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Peel the potatoes and cut them into strips
  2. Soak the potato strips in water for about 30 minutes
  3. Dry the potatoes very well with paper towels
  4. Fry the potatoes in hot oil until they are golden and crispy
  5. Add salt and serve HOT
