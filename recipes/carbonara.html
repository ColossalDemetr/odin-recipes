Carbonara

Home

Description

Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, black pepper, and cured meat. It is creamy, rich, and very simple to make.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Eggs
  • Pecorino Romano or Parmesan cheese
  • Guanciale, pancetta or bacon
  • Black pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water
  2. Cook the guanciale, pancetta, or bacon in a pan until crispy
  3. Mix the eggs, cheese, and black pepper in a bowl
  4. Drain the pasta and combine it with the hot meat
  5. Add the egg mixture and stir quickly until the sauce becomes creamy
