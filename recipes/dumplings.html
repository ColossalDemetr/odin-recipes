Dumplings

Home

Description

Dumplings are small pieces of dough filled with meat or vegetables. They are soft, tasty, and popular in many countries.

Ingredients

  • Dumpling wrappers
  • Ground pork or another filling
  • Cabbage
  • Ginger
  • Green onions or chives
  • Salt
  • Sesame oil

Steps

  1. Mix the cabbage with salt and let it sit for a few minutes
  2. Combine the filling ingredients in a bowl
  3. Put a small spoon of filling in the center of each wrapper
  4. Fold the wrappers and press the edges closed
  5. Boil, steam, or fry the dumplings until cooked through
